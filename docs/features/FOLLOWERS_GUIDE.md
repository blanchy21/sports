The follow/unfollow buttons are implemented in the **UserProfileModal** component. Here's how to use them:

### To Follow a User:
1. Click on any user's avatar or username in a post
2. A user profile modal will open
3. Click the "Follow" button in the modal
4. Approve the transaction in Hive Keychain
5. The button will update to "Unfollow"

### To See Your Followers/Following:
Currently, you can see the counts on your profile page at `/profile`.
